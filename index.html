Company Background

GEARTECH is an innovative student-founded startup with a mission to enhance motorcycle rider safety through practical design and smart technology. We believe that every rider deserves maximum protection without breaking the budget. Guided by research and user feedback, we develop helmets that not only meet safety standards, but are also comfortable, lightweight, and stylish for daily use. As a young and dedicated team, we continually improve our products through real-world testing, practical innovation, and community collaboration, ensuring that NeuroShield remains the top choice for riders who value both safety and quality.

We continuously refine our products through field testing and user feedback, ensuring that every helmet meets essential safety standards while remaining accessible to students, delivery riders, and daily commuters.

About Us

Our team consists of students passionate about engineering, safety, and real-world problem solving. We value creativity, teamwork, and continuous improvement. Customer feedback is central to our development process, helping us enhance our design with each batch.
